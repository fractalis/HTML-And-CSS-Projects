Iron Maiden - Brave New World

Review

Iron Maiden's "Brave New World" is a quintessential Maiden album - containing their distinct sound. Featuring terrific guitar solos, fantastic lyrics, and legendary drumming, "Brave New World" delivers on all fronts and is an instant Maiden classic.


5 / 5 Guitars

Iced Earth - Something Wicked This Way Comes

Review

Featuring the "Something Wicked" triology, "Something Wicked This Way Comes" is a great addition to Iced Earth's catalog. The Trilogy is the centerpiece of the album, featuring the story of Set Abominae - the Setian Messiah who serves as the catalyst for mankind's destruction, the story is rich with metaphor and events paralleling throughout history.


3 / 5 Guitars

Profit Unending's Lead Drummer Leaves Band
4 days ago

Profit Unending's Lead Drumer, Ken Summers, left the band this week citing creative differences. In an interview with Metallium, Summers said that "Due to the direction the band was taking, I felt it was best that I move on to my own projects." It's expected that Profit Unending will quickly find a new drummer ahead of their work on their upcoming album, "The Grave of Wisdom".
